Ananthagiri Hills

Ananthgiri has moderate waterfall of about 20- 25ft height. Thin streams of water falling from top of the hill. You have to climb down steep, ruff terrain to reach the bottom of the falls which seemed to be dangerous. Ananthagiri Hills, Telangana is a Hills & Mountains destination and is best experienced when visited during November, December, January, February and March as you can clear skies and cool weather here.
